Formatting Instructions

To format your text, use the following markers:

  • # before a line for Heading 1 (e.g., # Heading 1)
  • ## before a line for Heading 2 (e.g., ## Heading 2)
  • ### before a line for Heading 3 (e.g., ### Heading 3)
  • • at the beginning of lines for Unordered List items (e.g., • Item 1)
  • 1. at the beginning of lines for Ordered List items (e.g., 1. Item 1)
  • Just type normally for Paragraphs.
